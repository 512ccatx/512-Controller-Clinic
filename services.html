The Permanent Fix for Stick Drift

Tired of stick drift ruining your aim and your game? The standard analog sticks in most controllers use carbon components that physically rub together, making wear-and-tear (and eventual drift) inevitable.

We don't just replace your faulty stick with another one that's doomed to fail.

We offer a permanent solution. We upgrade your controller's original analog sticks with upgraded TMR joysticks. These advanced sticks use quantum mechanical magnetic fields to detect movement, meaning there are no physical parts rubbing together.

The result: No friction, no wear, and no more stick drift. Ever.
