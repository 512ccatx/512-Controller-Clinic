Turn Your Graveyard into Cash (or Credit)

Have a controller with stick drift, sticky buttons, or just gathering dust? Don't throw it away. We buy used and broken controllers to refurbish and harvest for parts.

1. Get a Quote

Fill out the form below. Tell us the model and what's wrong with it (be honest!). We'll email you a provisional offer.

2. Send it In

Ship it to us from the comfort of your home. We can email you a prepaid shipping label instantly, or we can mail you a box with a return label included

3. Get Paid

Once it passes inspection, we send you cash via CashApp/PayPal/Apple Pay OR give you extra value in Store Credit towards a repair or custom build.

⚠️ Buying Policy

We do NOT buy controllers with extensive water damage, fire damage, or severe pest infestation.

All offers are "Subject to Inspection." If we receive a controller that is in significantly worse condition than described, we reserve the right to adjust the offer or return the controller to you.
