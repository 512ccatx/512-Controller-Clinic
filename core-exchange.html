The Core Exchange Program

Skip the repair line. Get a freshly refurbished controller shipped to your door, then mail us your old one for a $35 refund.

1. Buy & Receive

Pay $90 upfront. We will ship a premium, refurbished controller to you within 48 hours so you can get right back to gaming.

2. Swap & Ship

Once your new controller arrives, place your old controller (the "core") into the exact same box. Attach the included prepaid return label and drop it in the mail within 7 days.

3. Get $35 Back

Once we receive and inspect your old controller, we will refund $35 back to your original payment method within 48 hours.

Core Condition Requirements

To qualify for the $35 refund, your returned core must meet the following guidelines:

✅ Acceptable

  • • Stick drift
  • • Unresponsive or sticky buttons
  • • Degraded or dead batteries
  • • Must be Original Equipment (Official Sony or Microsoft)
  • • Must be able to power on and connect to the console

❌ Unacceptable

  • • Physical damage (cracked, smashed, or missing shell pieces)
  • • Liquid or water damage
  • • Third-party or knock-off brands

*If a core is inspected and found to be in an unacceptable condition, no refund will be issued and the broken controller will be mailed back to you.

*Color & Revision Disclaimer: We cannot guarantee that the refurbished controller you receive will be the exact same color or board revision as the core you send in. However, if you email us before ordering to let us know what you currently have, we will do our absolute best to send you an identical match!
